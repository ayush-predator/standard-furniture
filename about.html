what we offer



we are standard furniture NX



The strong foundation of Standard Furniture NX was laid down in the year 2013 which emerged into market as a well-known brand. Located in Raipur, Standard Furniture NX has been in a prominent position in Chhattisgarh region as well as in the neighbouring state of Central India for more than 8 yr . We have been the manufacturer and supplier of all kinds of furniture for corporate Offices, Schools, Residence, Hospitals, Factories, Banks, Business Houses, etc.

We have multiple dealer network spread over different states in central India in order to remain easily accessible for our consumers. We aim to widen our reach all over the state and the country to become one of the top furniture brands of India.

The year 2013 holds an invaluable significance throughout journey of Standard Furniture NX, was established with an aim "To Make Human Life more Pleasant and Comfortable by Providing Valuable Solutions." The main Plant of the company is outspread in a wide area located at Mowa, Raipur. It is a fully equipped manufacturing unit with world-class infrastructure and technologies that produces complete range of furniture made from Wooden, Pre Laminated Particle Board, Acrylic, Veneer Finished, HDF, MDF, Solid Wood, etc. The company supplies all its products in wholesale and retail market pan India.


our present status



The enormous plant of Standard Furniture NX consists of dedicated furniture manufacturing facilities and having the latest equipment and technology to manufacture product confirming to BIFMA, ISO and Gem standards.

Today, It is one of the most renowned brand name in states like Chhattisgarh, MP, Maharashtra, Orrisa, Jharkhand, and Bihar.

Our factory is spread around 21000 sq. ft. area and have a production capacity of 5000 unit per year.. The group have over 100+ employs working consistently with us

From the beginning of its existence, Standard Furniture NX has been dedicated in delivering top-notch quality products and value added services. The group is continuously engaged in learning and adopting new technologies for producing high tech furniture and remain at the forefront of the industry. We love bringing alive your work space. We emphasise on comfort and aesthetics while delivering well-designed and functional furniture solution to you.
