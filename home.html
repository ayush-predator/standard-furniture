Welcome To SweetHut

Furnish Your Life

SweetHut is a brand of Standard Furniture NX was established with an aim "To Make Human Life more Pleasant and Comfortable by Providing Valuable Solutions." The main Plant of the company is outspread in a area located at Daldal Seoni, Mowa, Raipur C.G, India. It is a fully equipped manufacturing unit with world-class infrastructure and technologies that produces complete range of furniture made from Wooden, Pre Laminated Particle Board, Acrylic, Veneer Finished, HDF, MDF, Solid Wood, etc.
